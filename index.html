"democratizing heart sound diagnostics..."

Easy, rapid and highly accurate way of analyzing heart sounds on your mobile device, with Artificial Intelligence!

How does Steth.ai work?

Easy, rapid and accurate auscultation support in 3 steps:

 

  1. You can record a heart sound with a digital stethoscope or select an already recorded heart sound from your local drive,
  2. The selected heart sound is then sent to Steth.ai servers, where it is statistically assessed with a very large real-life database of heart sounds,
  3. Steth.ai deep learning algorithms finds the most likely diagnosis within seconds and returns it to your mobile device along with a confidence rate.

Steth.ai mobile app

  1. Pair any digital stethoscope via Bluetooth
  2. Save the recorded heart sound to your mobile device
  3. Visualize the heart sounds: view the phonocardiograms
  4. Analyze the heart sounds with our AI-based algorithm
  5. Detect and identify murmurs and other cardiac abnormalities
  6. Playback the recorded heart sounds
  7. Learn essential auscultation skills with audio samples

Steth.ai is here to empower you to auscultate with the same precision as a trained Cardiologist.

 

Steth.ai is proven to make a difference in the practice of medical professionals as it helps them to identify many cardiac diseases in the primary care setting.

Who uses Steth.ai?

Steth.ai is an AI-based heart sound diagnostic support system for mobile platforms. Steth.ai brings the common wisdom, experience and assesments skills of the best Cardiologists in the world to the physician’s stethoscope.
